# Member/Investor Dashboard

## Overview

The Member Dashboard enables individuals to discover, evaluate, and participate in Shariah-compliant investment opportunities through a Mudarabah contract with the platform. Investors can choose between directed investments (selecting specific businesses) or pooled investments (platform-managed diversification).

---

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Investment Modes

#### Directed Investment Mode
- Browse pre-approved business opportunities
- Select specific businesses for capital allocation
- Submit allocation requests to platform for approval
- Track individual business performance
- Maintain control over investment preferences

#### Pooled Investment Mode
- Deposit capital into general Mudarabah pool
- Platform manages diversified allocation
- Receive proportional share of overall platform performance
- Passive investment approach with professional management
- Lower minimum investment requirements
